Psychology

At the start of the pandemic, schools and businesses needed ways to encourage people to stay safe. Eventually, an organized system was put into place. Schools developed a hybrid school system where students would only come into school for part of the week. In school, students were required to wear masks and to stay at least six feet apart. Most businesses mandated masks in their building. Most stores also placed signs reminding customers to continue being safe throughout the building.
