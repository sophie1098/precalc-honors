Fun Facts

• Coronavirus is an umbrella term. Coronavirus is the family that many viruses fall under. For example, the common cold, SARS, and MERS all fall under the term coronavirus. COVID-19 is the specific disease, and the virus that causes COVID-19 is called SARS-CoV-2.

• SARS-CoV-2 lives longer depending on the surface. It lives for about 24 hours on cardboard and 2-3 days on metals like stainless steel.

• People with type A blood are more likely to get infected with coronavirus. The percentage of people who have tested positive for COVID-19 with type A blood is much higher than the percentage for other blood types. The opposite is true for people with type O blood. People infected with COVID-19 with type O blood are not as common as other blood types.

• Coronaviruses are sensitive to heat. High temperatures or increased levels of humidity can make the virus no longer viable.
